What we're thinking about

The world is accelerating.

AI will reshape labor, governance, and power. New governance models are emerging that challenge how we organize at scale. Technology is concentrating wealth and influence faster than institutions can adapt. Emerging markets are leapfrogging old models entirely. The next decade will be decided by people thinking ahead of these shifts.

Most institutions are reactive.

Universities study what already happened. Policy makers respond to problems that are already visible. Think tanks publish after the fact. We're building a community that works differently: we see signals early, we stress-test assumptions together, and we develop ideas that become real solutions.

You can be a builder, not a spectator.

The people shaping the future aren't waiting for permission. They're building. Thinking. Testing. Collaborating. The Fellowship is where those people work together on problems that matter.

How it works

We're not a traditional fellowship. We're a collective of thinkers, builders, and practitioners working at the edge of what's possible.

Fellowship

You're part of a community of 50-75 fellows globally. You get a seat at the table where ideas are being tested and real solutions are being built.

Access to research: Early papers, working drafts, data on emerging issues

Quarterly convenings: In-person and virtual gatherings where we work on specific problems

Collaborative projects: Work alongside other fellows on ideas that could scale

The network: 500+ people across founders, policy makers, technologists, and impact leaders

What comes next

The goal isn't theory. It's action. Fellows have launched startups, published policy recommendations, built governance prototypes, and created real solutions.

Recent projects: Agromovil (connecting smallholder farmers to markets), AI governance frameworks for emerging economies, new cooperative models tested with real organizations

Impact: 15,000+ producers reached, $16M+ in value created, policies influencing EU AI governance

The Fellowship Method

We use structured foresight, scenario planning, and collaborative problem-solving. We read weak signals before they become obvious. We stress-test ideas in real systems. We connect people who would never find each other otherwise. And we move quickly—from idea to prototype to real-world testing in months, not years.

Who we're looking for

Builders

Founders, technologists, engineers. People who move from idea to action. You don't need to have succeeded—you need to be willing to try.

Thinkers

Researchers, policy architects, strategists. People who see patterns others miss. You're comfortable with uncertainty and ambiguity.

Connectors

Investors, mentors, institutional leaders. People with networks and resources who want to help others solve big problems.

Impact Leaders

Social entrepreneurs, global development professionals, governance reformers. People working on the problems we care about.

Futurists

People trained in foresight, scenario planning, and strategic anticipation. You help others see what's coming.

Lifetime Learners

People curious about everything. You read across domains, you ask hard questions, you're willing to be wrong.

You don't need a credential. You need conviction. You need to believe that the future can be better—and you're willing to do the work to build it.
