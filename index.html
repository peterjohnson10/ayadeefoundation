How we engage

We don't sell frameworks off the shelf. We build custom work for leaders who move capital and shape institutions. Here's what that looks like.

01

Initial Diagnostic

We sit down with your leadership to understand your positioning, your competitive blind spots, and where you're exposed to major shifts in technology, governance, or markets.

4-6 weeks. Delivered as a clear brief and candid board-level conversation.

02

Strategy Workshops

Half-day sessions, or multi-day immersions where your team steps back from operations to think systemically. We use scenario planning and structured analysis to test your assumptions and explore options.

Ongoing. Quarterly or as needed.

03

Method Transfer

You learn the actual work. How to read policy signals. How to spot governance innovations. How to think about wealth transitions. We teach what we use so you can keep doing this yourself.

Standalone modules or integrated with strategy work.

04

Ongoing Partnership

For decisions that truly matter, you want external eyes. Quarterly conversations, ongoing access to our research and networks, and guidance on your highest-stakes moves. We become your external brain.

12-month minimum, custom terms.

Every engagement connects you to our network of researchers, former fellows, and domain experts across governance, technology, and development work.
