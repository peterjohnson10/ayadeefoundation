How we work

No courses. No generic frameworks. We offer an integrated engagement model designed for executives who move capital and influence.

01

Strategic Diagnostic

We conduct a structured conversation with your leadership to identify gaps between current positioning and emerging opportunities in AI, governance, markets, and social change.

4-6 weeks. Delivered as a brief document and board-level conversation.

02

Experiential Strategy Sessions

Half-day to multi-day immersive workshops where your team learns to think in systems. We use scenario planning, signal interpretation, and structured imagination to stress-test your assumptions.

Ongoing. Quarterly or as-needed engagements.

03

Proprietary Methods & Tools

We teach you the tools we use to read signals in AI policy, emerging governance models, and wealth transitions. You become self-sufficient in foresight work.

Available as standalone modules or integrated into strategy engagements.

04

Strategic Retainer

Ongoing access to our research, quarterly strategy conversations, and facilitation for your most critical decisions. We become your external strategic counsel.

Custom terms. Usually 12-month minimum.

Every engagement includes access to our Ayadee Foundation Fellowship Network—current fellows, mentors, and industry experts across emerging tech, governance, and development.
