How to Use These Banners

  1. For Google Forms: Take a screenshot of the wide banner above. Open your Google Form → Settings → Customize theme → Upload image as header.
  2. For Email/Social: Screenshot any banner and resize as needed. Share across your network.
  3. For Landing Page: Copy the HTML code for any banner and add to a custom page before your form link.
  4. For Custom Use: Right-click any banner → "Save image as" → Use in presentations, documents, or announcements.
